# 🎨 Aegis CFO — Motion Graphics Prompts (per scene)

Tool-agnostic prompts for AI video generators (Sora, Google Veo, Kling, Runway Gen-3, Pika) or a motion designer.
Each scene = a copy-paste prompt + on-screen text + the cut it belongs to.

---

## 🌐 GLOBAL STYLE GUIDE (paste this prefix into every prompt for consistency)

> **Style:** premium fintech motion graphics, dark mode, cinematic, clean and minimal, glassmorphism + subtle holographic UI, thin geometric lines, soft depth-of-field, volumetric glow, 4K, 24fps, smooth eased motion (no jitter), shallow parallax.
> **Color palette:** deep midnight navy `#0B1220` background, electric teal/cyan accent `#16E0C8`, warm gold `#F5C451` reserved ONLY for money/value highlights, soft white `#E8EEF5` text.
> **Brand motif:** a glowing hexagonal **shield** (the "aegis") and thin "guardrail" rails/locks.
> **Typography:** bold modern grotesque sans-serif (Inter / Söhne style), generous spacing.
> **Mood:** confident, trustworthy, fast but calm. Aspect ratio 16:9.

## ① PROBLEM STATEMENT — *0:00–0:25*
**Concept:** chaos → order. Paper invoices and spreadsheet cells swirl in dark space, overwhelming; everything freezes as a question appears.

> **PROMPT:** [GLOBAL STYLE] Cinematic dark scene: hundreds of translucent paper invoices, receipts, and glowing spreadsheet grid-cells float and tumble chaotically through midnight-navy space, lit by cold cyan rim-light, papers stamped "PENDING" and red "?" marks drifting past camera. Slow dolly-in through the swirling documents, shallow depth of field, dust particles in the light. Halfway, the chaos slows to a freeze-frame and dims. Mood: overwhelming, tense. Subtle low drone audio feel. Camera: slow push-in then settle.
>
> **ON-SCREEN TEXT (fades in over the freeze):** *"What if your CFO never slept — and never went rogue?"*

Particles settle into a faint geometric grid floor. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the emblem centered in clean dark space. Mood: arrival, relief, confidence. Smooth eased motion, lens flare on ignition.

## ④ DEMO 2 — AUDIT (it *protects*) — *1:25–2:05*
**Concept:** the council + the catch. Five glowing persona orbs deliberate around a ledger; a scan sweeps the books and snaps red on two fraudulent transactions.

> **PROMPT:** [GLOBAL STYLE] Five softly glowing teal orb-avatars arranged in a semicircle around a floating translucent ledger table of transaction rows; thin light-threads pulse between the orbs as they "deliberate." A horizontal cyan scan-line sweeps down the ledger rows; most rows flash green "✓ matched", then the scan LOCKS onto two rows that flash alarm-red and lift off the table: one tagged "ROGUE CHARGE $450 — no record", the other "OVERBILL +$300". A second translucent "Stripe" ledger appears beside the first and light-lines connect matching rows (reconciliation), the two red rows left visibly unmatched. Camera slowly pushes toward the two red rows. Mood: vigilant, forensic, satisfying catch.
>
> **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** *"5-persona audit council"* → *"Reconciled against Stripe"* → **"2 exceptions caught automatically"**
> **TRANSITION:** intercut with real `/audit` report screen recording showing the two highlighted exceptions.

## ⑤ THE BIG IDEA / MOAT — *2:05–2:25*
**Concept:** the thesis as a visual. A split: a free-flowing "mind" of light vs. a locked "hands" rail — freedom to think, constraint to act.

> **PROMPT:** [GLOBAL STYLE] Split-composition motion graphic. LEFT half: an expansive, freely-swirling neural cloud of teal light-particles and branching thought-lines, unbounded and alive (cognition). The deterministic side shows crisp locked numeric digits snapping into place precisely (never flickering), while the cognition side stays fluid. Then both halves harmonize into the hexagonal shield. Camera: locked, elegant. Mood: balanced, principled, trustworthy.
>
> **ON-SCREEN TEXT (bold, center):** **"Constrain actions. Free cognition."**

## 🔧 PRODUCTION NOTES
- **Generate each clip 5–8s long**, then trim/speed to fit the timings above. AI video tools drift past ~8s — keep prompts to single continuous moves.
- **Keep text OUT of the AI generation** (models garble letters). Generate clean motion-graphic plates, then add all text/captions in your editor (CapCut, Premiere, DaVinci Resolve — all free-tier capable).
- **Cohesion trick:** always lead with the GLOBAL STYLE block so palette + shield motif repeat across every clip.
- **Audio:** one calm-but-driving electronic track, ducked under voiceover; add a soft "whoosh" on each scene transition and a subtle "click/lock" SFX on the guardrail moments (③ and ⑤).
- **Hybrid is best:** scenes ①②⑤⑥ = pure motion graphics; scenes ③④ = motion-graphic intro card → real screen recording. That mix reads as both polished AND real to judges.
